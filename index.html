Greatest Anime

Death Note

After discovering a notebook capable of killing people when their names are written into it, the series revolves around a highly intelligent high school student, Light Yagami

Black clover

Asta and Yuno are orphans raised in the outskirts of the Clover Kingdom.The story follows Asta and Yuno as they crawl their way up the ladder of the Clover Kingdom's Order of the Magic Knights.

Attack On Titans

In the story, humanity stands on the edge of extinction for being attacked by the titans. The main characters Eren Yeager and Mikasa Ackerman with the other Survey Corps members battle against the titans.

Naruto

The series revolves around a fictional world of Shinobi, where Naruto learns to become a ninja master with the help of his friends and other masters.

Demon Slayer

It follows Tanjiro Kamado and his sister Nezuko Kamado as they seek a cure for Nezuko's demon curse.

Tokyo Revengers

When Takemichi is pushed in front of a train, he teleports exactly 12 years into the past to 2005.
